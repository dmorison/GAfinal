About Commuting

Commute: travel some distance between one's home and place of work on a regular basis. -Oxford English Dictionary

A fun (rather random) illustration comparing the speed at which we commute at to animals while also highlighting the diversity of commuting around the world with some fun facts. (All packaged in a fully responsive webpage demonstrating some uses of JavaScript and jQuery)

The world average commuting time for people who work is 40 minutes (one-way). Thialand has the worlds longest commuting time with the average working person spending 2 hours commuting per day, while Malawi has the shortest commuting times of around 2 minutes (one-way). -World Mapper
According to the 2011 IBM Commuter Pain Survey, Mexico City scored the highest.
The average London (UK) worker spends 1 hour 22 minutes per day commuting, while 9% of the UK work force spends at least 3 hours commuting per day! -The Daily Mail
